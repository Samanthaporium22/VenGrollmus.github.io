Personal Information

I was born in Sydney, and have what they call a heinz variety background. My father is German, and on my mum's father side of the family is Chinese and Aboriginal. On mum's mum side of the family we go back to England.

I started working at Woolies back in the day where price guns were still being used and it was my job to do the weekly specials each Saturday, and price the stock. During this time, I also was in the Army Reserves in Victoria as an Engineer (Sapper) with the 7th Field Engineer Regiment.

After working for Telecom/Telstra for nearly 25 years and then another business for a couple of years, I now work for myself providing administrative support, help desk services, and develop websites and applications for a number of clients across Australia and New Zealand.
